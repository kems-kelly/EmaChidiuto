About Me

I'm a UX Engineer with a passion for building thoughtful, scalable design systems and products. With 4+ years of experience at leading tech companies, I've developed a deep understanding of how design and engineering intersect to create meaningful user experiences.

My approach combines strategic thinking with meticulous attention to detail. I believe great products emerge from the intersection of user empathy, systems thinking, and elegant implementation. Whether architecting design systems, prototyping new interactions, or optimizing complex workflows, I'm driven by the pursuit of craft and impact.

Currently, I'm exploring the intersection of AI and design, helping teams navigate the unique challenges of designing for intelligent systems. I'm always interested in discussing design systems, product strategy, and the future of human-computer interaction.
